About Us



Cardiovascular diseases, encompassing conditions such as coronary heart disease, stroke, peripheral arterial disease, and aortic disease, stand as a significant global health challenge, accounting for a substantial portion of morbidity and mortality. Early and accurate diagnosis of these diseases is pivotal in facilitating timely medical intervention and improving patient outcomes. In this context, the integration of deep learning and image processing techniques has emerged as a promising for non-invasive and efficient disease detection from electrocardiogram (ECG) images.

In the diagnosing these cardiovascular diseases often involved invasive procedures, such as angiography or surgical interventions. The Inception v3 deep learning algorithm, which is renowned for its accuracy and efficiency in image classification tasks.

The power of deep learning and image processing, we aim to enhance the precision and speed of cardiovascular disease diagnosis while minimizing the need for invasive procedures. This research endeavors to contribute to the growing body of knowledge aimed at improving the early diagnosis of these life-threatening conditions, ultimately enhancing patient care, reducing healthcare costs, and increasing the overall well-being of individuals affected by cardiovascular diseases.
